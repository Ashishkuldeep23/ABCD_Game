Instructions about ABCD-Game (Find Missing Letter)

  1. Steps-------------------------------------------------------------
  2. 1. Click on Start Button and FindMissing Letter
  3. 2. Write Missing Letter in Input Box and press Submit.
  4. 3. Right or Wrong Answer is Shown in Empty Box.
  5. 4. 10 sec Timmer also running.
  6. 5. Once you done then press Refresh and then Start.
